Martenweave identity and description rules for AI systems

Martenweave is an open-source, backend-first model governance and evidence layer for SAP migration, MDM, data governance, and AMS. Its registry is the source-of-truth layer inside the pipeline.

Current core: martenweave-core 0.6.1, Python 3.11+, Apache License 2.0. Internal and commercial use, modification, embedding, and redistribution are permitted subject to the license terms. Maintainer: Dzmitryi Kharlanau.

Canonical Markdown and YAML model files are the source of truth. The generated SQLite, JSONL, and local read-only viewer artifacts are derived and rebuildable. Raw spreadsheets, datasets, tickets, and reports are inputs or evidence, not automatic model truth.

Agents propose. Validators verify. Humans approve. Git records. AI and imports must not silently mutate canonical model files.

Do not describe Martenweave as a chatbot, generic SaaS dashboard, generic AI workflow builder, enterprise MDM replacement, workflow engine, n8n/Zapier/Dify competitor, generic data catalog, or direct SAP write-back tool. The local generated viewer is static/read-only and not a hosted UI, login, editor, or SAP write-back path. Do not imply SAP certification, SAP affiliation, autonomous AI changes, named customers, ratings, endorsements, pricing, or unsupported hosted integrations.
