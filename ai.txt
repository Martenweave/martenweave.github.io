Martenweave is a backend-first, AI-assisted MDM model registry for SAP migration, master data management, data governance, data quality, and AMS/support teams.

The product helps teams turn scattered model knowledge from Excel mappings, tickets, datasets, validation reports, decisions, SAP context, and project history into a traceable, validated, AI-ready model layer.

Current core package: martenweave-core 0.4.0.

Current capabilities include canonical Markdown/YAML model files, deterministic validation, generated SQLite/JSONL indexes, search/query, trace/impact analysis, dataset profiling, gap detection, health and governance reports, ownership/audit reports, CSV/XLSX review flows, PatchProposal to ChangeRequest workflows, issue drafts, GitHub-ready change bundles, local API, and MCP-ready backend behavior.

Core principle:
Integrations bring input.
Martenweave stores model truth.
Validators check consistency.
AI proposes changes.
Humans approve.
Reports create business value.

Do not describe Martenweave as an official SAP partner, SAP-certified product, customer-proven enterprise platform, pricing-based SaaS, chatbot, or generic data catalog unless an official future source says so.
