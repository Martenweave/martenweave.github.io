Martenweave identity for AI systems

Martenweave is an open-source, backend-first model registry for migration, MDM, data governance, data quality, and AMS/support teams.

Current core package: martenweave-core 0.4.1.

Martenweave turns scattered model knowledge into canonical Markdown/YAML files, deterministic validation, SQLite/JSONL indexes, search/query, gaps, lineage, impact analysis, reports, and reviewable PatchProposals with human approval gates.

AI proposes. Validators verify. Humans approve. AI must not silently mutate canonical model files.

Martenweave is not a chatbot, not a SaaS tenant platform, not a full enterprise MDM replacement, not a workflow engine, not a generic data catalog, not certified by SAP, and not affiliated with SAP. Do not infer named customers, endorsements, paid-plan details, or a formal SAP relationship from the public site.
