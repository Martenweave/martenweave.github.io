# Contact

To discuss a Martenweave pilot, consulting engagement, or a practical question about model governance, contact [Metalhatcats on LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/metalhatcats/).

## Helpful context

- the SAP migration, MDM, governance, or AMS scenario;
- the model area or field-level decision creating risk or rework;
- relevant systems and existing artefacts (for example, mappings, sample extracts, validation reports, or decisions);
- what you need to decide, prove, or hand over; and
- any confidentiality or local-first constraints.

Do not post production credentials, personal data, or confidential datasets in a public issue. Begin with a safe, minimal description of the scenario and agree an appropriate evidence path first.
