# Dzmitryi Kharlanau

Dzmitryi Kharlanau is a Senior SAP Consultant at EPAM Systems, working across SAP logistics, MDG, AMS, integration, and data. He has more than 12 years of experience in SAP SD, logistics, integration, MDG-related work, and AMS-heavy delivery.

His work covers SAP S/4HANA support and enhancement delivery, ABAP-backed troubleshooting, integration and interface diagnosis, master-data governance, and AI-ready support knowledge systems. Martenweave applies that operational perspective to migration and governance work: canonical model files, deterministic validation, traceable evidence, and human-approved change.

## Areas of focus

- SAP SD, logistics, and order-to-cash
- SAP MDG, master-data governance, and migration
- SAP AMS, integration, and interface troubleshooting
- ABAP-backed analysis, validation, lineage, and impact analysis
- AI-ready operational knowledge and controlled AI-assisted change

## Martenweave

Martenweave turns scattered model knowledge into canonical files, deterministic validation results, generated indexes, and reviewable change proposals. Its Core is available under the Apache License 2.0.
