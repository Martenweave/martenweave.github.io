# Dzmitryi Kharlanau

Dzmitryi Kharlanau is the maintainer of Martenweave, an open-source, local-first model registry for SAP migration, MDM, data governance, and controlled AI-assisted model change.

## Areas of focus

- SAP migration and master data management
- Data governance and canonical data-model design
- Deterministic validation, lineage, and impact analysis
- Human-approved AI-assisted model change

## Martenweave

Martenweave turns scattered model knowledge into canonical files, deterministic validation results, generated indexes, and reviewable change proposals. Its Core is available under the Apache License 2.0.

For professional updates, connect on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dkharlanau/). For the product and source, see the [Martenweave documentation](/docs.html) and [Martenweave organization on GitHub](https://github.com/Martenweave).
