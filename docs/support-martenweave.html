Support Martenweave

Martenweave Core is open source under the Apache License 2.0. Support helps move practical model-governance work forward: reusable scenarios, starter packs, validation rules, examples, documentation, and local-first integration surfaces.

Ways to support

Sponsor a roadmap conversation

If your organisation wants to shape a roadmap area around a real migration, MDM, governance, or AMS problem, sponsor a focused discovery conversation. This is a way to make a concrete need visible; it does not buy control of project decisions or create a closed fork by default.

Become a design partner

Bring a representative, approved-to-share challenge: a model slice, mapping workbook, recurring incident pattern, or governance decision. The collaboration can turn that experience into reusable patterns while keeping client-specific evidence confidential.

Commission a pilot or focused engagement

Start with a bounded pilot project to establish evidence, canonical structure, validation, gaps or impact analysis, and a review path around one real decision. For a broader working model, see consulting and the engagement process.

Contribute in public

Useful contributions are not limited to code. You can share reproducible scenarios, fixture-model needs, documentation improvements, issue reports, or feedback on a governance workflow. Read the open-source guide and contributing scenarios to find the right path.

What support does not change

  • Canonical model files remain the source of truth.
  • AI-assisted work stays proposal-first and human-approved.
  • Client evidence and confidential delivery material are not published as a condition of collaboration.
  • Open-source licensing and public contribution terms remain visible and separate from any private engagement.

Start a conversation

A public sponsorship platform is not currently configured. The best first step is to describe the problem, the model area, and the result you need.
